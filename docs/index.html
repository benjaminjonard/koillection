Koillection

A self-hosted website to manage all your collections

With Koillection you can manage:



Collections

Items

Signs

Tags

Wishlists

Manage
With Koillection you are free to manage your collections as you want.

Share
You can choose to share your collections with other people, while retaining full control on what they will be able to see.

Invite
Other people can be invited to manage their collections independently on your server.

Open-source
Koillection is completely open-source and can be found on Github.
You are free to use, edit and improve the code.
Any help or suggestions are welcome.

Private
Everything is stored on your own server, you have full control over your data and anything you upload.

PWA
Koillection is a PWA (Progressive Web App).
It means it can be used on your smartphone or tablet like any other app, giving you a seemless experience.
